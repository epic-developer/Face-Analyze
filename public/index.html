CFQI
Cohort Face Query Interface
HOW TO USE




NOTICE: Upload a close up photo of ONE face. To optimize results, ensure the face fills the entire frame, improve lighting, present a neutral expression, stand against a plain backdrop, and remove any makeup, headphones, or other accessories.
URL of Webpage with Images to Search for Face:   
AI Sensitivity (OPTIONAL): (0.1 to 0.9)   
Lower values will increase sensitivity which will make the AI more strict on matching features between faces. This may result in more accuracy but at the cost of omitting some potentially valid comparisons.

Photosensitive Epileptic Warning: When scanning large data sets, images may appear to flash onto the screen as faces are continuously rapidly displayed and removed as the AI scans them.
